Nowadays our quality of life is an increasing concern, our standards only get higher and we expect more and more of the seemingly limitless world of technology. But are our special needs getting attended?

Although there's a plethora of applications that provide, for example, text-to-speech functionalities for the blind, the market still lacks a simple, intuitive solution that provides a meaningful help to warn us about allergens in the food products, while offering all the personalized care for people with special needs.

We present to you ARPA (Allergic Reaction Product Alert), an application with a set of features focused on accessibility, which are intended to assist the user in choosing which food products won't contain harmful allergens. ARPA will help you pick what products you should buy based on your, and your family's, allergies!

Insight is an email client for desktop with the purpose of improving the email experience. Insight's main features are:
  • Automatic inbox sorting, allowing the user to know the subject of an email before reading its title.
  • Information filtering, reducing the mail content to the bare essential, allowing a more fluid reading experience.
  • Actions over emails, allowing the execution of automatic actions according to the email's information, saving time and increasing productivity.
Beamian is a powerful event management platform that leverages the power of the NFC technology. The platform supports your event in four distinct stages:
  • Attraction: Reinforce the event innovation positioning by making the register process easier, integrating it with social media networks and marketing it via Email and SMS.
  • Welcoming: Deliver an outstanding first impression with an user-friendly check-in system.
  • Engagement: Create a unique experience through tools that facilitate the exchange of contacts and improve the involvement between exhibitors, organizers and visitors.
  • Follow-up: The platform allows real time interaction, improving visitor experience, and it will output valuable behaviour information for you to follow-up maximizing the return of each visitor, which will deliver the correct information to the correct person, increasing sales and the return on investment of every event.
Consumer and trade fairs should be an opportunity to bring people closer to the companies that make their lives better. However, this is not always the case - complicated showrooms, coupled with large crowds and exhibition booths that lack in appeal often make people avoid interaction.

So, we bring you our Live Fair App - a mobile application that supports visitors, companies and organizers during these events, saving up time for what really matters: social interaction. Our main features include:
  • Helpful overview of upcoming fairs, with interest and location based suggestions
  • Detailed information about fairs, their event schedule and participating companies
  • Interest-based matching between visitors and companies
  • Helpful statistics for participating companies and fair organizers
  • Comprehensive notifications about upcoming events

Keep it simple and never again get lost in a busy, cumbersome event. Let us guide you. Let us bring life to your fair.
